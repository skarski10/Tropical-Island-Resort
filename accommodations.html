Amenities and Services

At the Tropical Island Resort each room is uniquely designed for a different experience each visit. Whether you are staying in our hotel or one of our beachfront bures, you are sure to enjoy your stay.

Hotel Rooms

Each room includes the following:

  • Wifi
  • Mini Bar
  • Outdoor Balcony
  • Rainhead showers
  • Beach towels
  • Robes
  • Air conditioning
  • Ceiling Fans
  • Room safe
  • Refridgerator
  • Coffe maker
  • Pillow Menus
  • Daily housekeeping

Bures

Bures include all ammenities included in hotel room with the addition of the following:

  • Glass floors
  • Window screens
  • Outdoor patio
  • Umbrellas
  • King-Sized beds
